    Informations

    => Why was it created?

    Books are present in all our lives and therefore play a very important role in everyone. So, it is extremely important that EVERYONE's stories are told, no matter who they are or how they are. We are all human and we are all the main characters in our stories! In this way, we bring stories in which the minority can feel represented.

    => How can I help?

    Contact Us! Send us your work and our team will read it and, if you comply with the rules, add it to our portfolio.

    Rules

    Stories must always follow 3 rules:

    Rule 1

    Make sure to always respect all human rights!

    Rule 2

    Never write something that makes readers feel uncomfortable!

    Rule 3

    Never offend any group, community or person!
